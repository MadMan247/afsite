Mellow: Out Now!

Acid Fog's Mellow EP is finally out.

Mellow Album Artwork

...

Mellow, released on September 20th 2023, is a beautiful and mellow EP full of sweet and soft songs combined with heavier elements. The title track, Mellow, is the 4th track in the EP The EP ends with a simple piano tune (Fading Memories) performed by Ethan and Connor. The track list is as follows:

  1. Lost In Time
  2. Away From Home
  3. Pictures Of Clouds
  4. Mellow
  5. Disappear
  6. Fading Memories
