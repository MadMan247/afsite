Into The Fog: Out Now!

The FIRST Official album from Acid Fog is now availible to listen anywhere you can find good music.

Into The Fog Album and Information Sheet

...

Released on April 20th, 2023, Into The Fog is an album containing seven tracks, the Title Track "Into The Fog" is the second track on the album. The track list is as follows:

  1. The Acid King
  2. Into The Fog
  3. The Trip
  4. Psychotic Break From Reality
  5. Memory Banks
  6. The Gentle Return to Sanity
  7. Shoelacer

The tracks 4-6 are seamlessly transitioning songs, meant to be enjoyed one after the other, but they are also able to be listened to independantly without sounding like part is missing.

This album was described in Activator Magazine's September 2023 issue as "...a well-balanced experience, offering a combination of heavy riffs and peaceful, somber grooves."
